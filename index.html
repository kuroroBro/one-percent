How to play
  • Everyone answers the same multiple-choice question (2-4 options) at once, in secret — no one can see anyone else's pick until it's revealed.
  • Each question shows the percentage of people who supposedly got it right when it was surveyed. The first question is The Line, usually the easiest.
  • Answer wrong, or don't answer before the timer runs out, and you're eliminated.
  • If everyone still standing gets a question wrong together, the whole group falls off the line and no one wins.
  • Survive every question on the ladder — all the way down to the hardest one available — and you've reached the 1%. More than one player can clear it together.
  • No accounts, no ads, no tracking. One device per player, plus a host who controls the pace — the host can play along too, or run the room as a hands-off shared display.
